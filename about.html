Our Story

I started Simple Consulting because I was tired of seeing good businesses get stuck in messy systems, bad processes, and confusing tech. E-commerce doesn’t have to be complicated—but too often, it is. I’ve spent years in the trenches working with Shopify, Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and cross-border logistics. I’ve seen what works, what breaks, and what quietly kills margins. Simple Consulting is my way of taking all of that experience and using it to help brands grow smarter, not harder. My goal is simple: build clean systems, clear processes, and real results—so you can focus on growing your business without fighting your backend every day.
